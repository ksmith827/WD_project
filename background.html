Hillary Clinton

Background

When Hillary Clinton was elected to the U.S. Senate in 2001,
she became the first American first lady to ever win a public
office seat. She later became the 67th U.S. secretary of
state in 2009, serving until 2013. In 2016, she became the
first woman in U.S. history to become the presidential nominee
of a major political party.
